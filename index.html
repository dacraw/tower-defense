Instructions:

Use the Upgrade Income button on the left to increase your resource generation rate. Each time you do this, it will increase the cost of the next upgrade.

Resources generate automatically. Use them to buy and upgrade towers, as well as increase resource generation rate.

Add towers by clicking on the tower type. The cost is listed below.

Your score increases as you deal more damage.

Level increments after all enemies are cleared.

Health decreases each time an enemy reaches your base.

Enemies decrease when their health reaches 0 or they reach your base.
